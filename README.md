## Challenge

Develop a comprehensive strategy to enhance operational efficiency for a metallurgical company through process optimization, cost reduction, and digital transformation initiatives.

---

## Solution

Our team proposed a **three-component roadmap** for production digitalization:

### 1. MES (Manufacturing Execution System)
- Real-time monitoring of production lines
- Equipment load control and schedule optimization
- Integration with quality management systems

### 2. ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)
- Centralized procurement, warehouse, and financial management
- Automated demand planning and forecasting
- Cross-departmental business process synchronization

### 3. IIoT (Industrial Internet of Things)
- Sensor network for equipment condition monitoring
- **Predictive Maintenance**: failure prediction analytics
- **Quality Control**: automated defect detection
